A little bit about me:

Assistant Professor of Education Leadership

I'll be teaching a couple of classes in the fall at Lewis University. This will be my first year there and I'm excited to be a part of this great Catholic university and its Education Leadership department.

Research Experience

My dissertation examined citizenship education for refugee students in IB schools. In addition, I have published work on citizenship education in post-conflict contexts, with a focus on West Africa. My research interests include refugee education, education for global citizenship, global education, the IB movement, literacy acquisition in a second or foreign language, and assessment or expectations of immigrant and refugee students.

Conferencees
